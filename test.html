Browser RPG Game

Choose your class!

Thorian Knight

Humanoid entities blessed with energies from
mana infused crystals, Thorian Knights are
adaptive fighters that are comfortable
in both close-quarters combat (sword and shield),
and ranged combat (spell casting).

Prism Walker

Born from the dark plains of Azteroth,
Prism Walkers are extremely powerful spell casters,
capable of manipulating time and space (teleportation),
and disintegrating their foes with destructive magic.

Bone Guard

Comprised of multiple spirits that inhabit the armor
of the fallen, Bone Guard are melee-focused warriors
incapable of casting spells or using ranged weapons.
In light of these shortcomings, however, Bone Guard
inherit a permanent life-steal bonus of 5%.
